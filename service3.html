Corporate Finance

Corporate Finance enables our clients in rediscovering their business’ true potential. We facilitate growth in the changing business environment by providing high quality solutions along with handholding. Team consists of top quality professionals with experience in a plethora of areas.

We use our extensive experience in capital raising projects; provide advice on deal structuring, negotiation and project management for our clients. We help our clients make some of their most important decisions using bespoke financial models. Our business modelling expertise lies in the ability to deduce the essence of the issues faced by the business and delivering insightful analysis. We differentiate our business by allocating the required time and resources on every engagement that we undertake. The over-riding goal of all our engagements is to assist you in enhancing business and stakeholder value.

- Budgeting and finance strategy – Accurate budgets for effective controls
- Business modelling – Developing comprehensive model paying attention to fine aspects
- Cash flow management – Devising a plan and ensuring smooth cash flow cycles
- Project evaluation & return assessment – Logic and rational analysis covering key aspects of project and arriving at return for the investor
- Raising Debt & equity – with successful track record, represent and support the client throughout the funding process
- Valuation - “Logic based” approach and ensuring sound, valid and verified reasoning behind the business value.
- Due diligence – Rational exercise on highlighting the factors which has the potential to impact the business and its valuation
- Entry and Exists – advisory services for buyers and sellers on their strategies and effective decision making
